Toffee Shortbread

Description

This is a scottish recipe also known as "Millionare's Shorbread" made with toffee and chocolate on top of shortbread. It's a perfect gift to hand out during christmas or to new neighbors!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • softened butter
  • sugar
  • chocolate
  • vanilla extract
  • you'll need: candy thermometer

    • Steps

    1. Combine flour, butter, and sugar in a bowl
    2. Knead dough until well combined, then put in fridge for 2 hours
    3. When dough is properly cooled, spread evenly into a greased pan and bake at 375 degrees for 9-12 minutes until edges are browned
    4. Heat butter and sugar in a saucepan until candy reaches 425 degrees
    5. Pour candy/toffee mix on cooled shortbread
    6. Melt chocolate (combine with coconut oil to help with the melting process) and pour on top of hardened toffee.
    7. Serve when cooled.
