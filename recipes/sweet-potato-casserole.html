Sweet Potato Casserole

Description

A sweet and creamy sweet potato casserole topped with crunchy pecans and brown sugar.

Ingredients

  • sweet potatoes
  • heavy cream
  • eggs
  • vanilla
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • pecans

    • Steps

    1. Peel and boil sweet potatoes until soft
    2. Mush sweet potatoes and combine in large bowl with heavy cream, eggs, butter, and vanilla
    3. Transfer mix into dish container and spread evenly
    4. Combine excess butter, brown sugar, and pecans in small bowl
    5. Spread brown sugar and pecan mix evenly over casserole mix
    6. Bake in oven at 400 degrees for 13-18 minutes until top is slightly browned
    7. Wait for casserole to cool slightly and serve
